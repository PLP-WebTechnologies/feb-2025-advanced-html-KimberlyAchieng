Welcome to PETPALS

Step up as a PawPal, make tails wag—volunteer and be a pet’s best tag!

How To Volunteer at PAWPALS

  1. Sign Up with us on our website through the provided form
  2. Choose a Role!
    • Pet Walker - Come in and take them on a walk
    • Pet Cuddler - Come in, pet them, feed them, and interact with them
    • Pet Groomer Conduit - Help our lovely pets get groomed by donating spare change
  3. Commit to Shifts – Schedule and attend regular volunteer sessions.
  4. Follow Guidelines – Adhere to shelter rules for safety and care.

Make a Difference! Join us in making a difference in the lives of pets in need. Your time and love can change their world!
